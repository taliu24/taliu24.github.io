Our Office

We offer only the best at Farrell and Farrell Eyecare. Our office is comfortable and inviting because our patient's care is very important to us. Our goal is to provide only the best for our patients and we hope that each visit is a wonderful experience. We carry a wide selection of designer eyewear with options for UV protection, Blue Block, anti-reflective, scratch resistant lenses to address your vision needs. We offer most brands of contact lenses, with options for disposable, rigid gas permeable, toric (astigmatic) and bifocal lenses. With the finest service, latest technology and a friendly staff, we believe we can provide our patients with the care they deserve.
